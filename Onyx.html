Onyx White Pearl, Tambon Rawai, Phuket

Beautiful, modern 3 bedrooms Villa Onyx White Pearl is a superb private semidetached villa with outdoor pool and garden. If you are looking for a nice cozy place within a 5 minute walk to restaurants and bars, yet private and in a peaceful setting then look no further! - 2 bedrooms + 1 detached bedroom (guesthouse) in the garden, each room with own bathroom.


A modern and cozy ambiance combining the heavenly tranquil of its lush and spacious grounds with all the comforts and modern commodities. As our guests, you will enjoy the exclusive comfort that comes alive with beauty of the place and wonderful Thailand beaches and famous night life. Nearby, Nai Harn Beach is one of the most beautiful and quiet beaches of the island. Many cafes and restaurants of national and European cuisine. Near the famous Fish Market Rawai. Close to fishing village and lots of seafood restaurants and other cuisines, massage, night market, bike and car hire all close by.


Villa has its own private walled garden and electric gate to a private driveway access and is located in a quiet and comfortable place south of the island in residential area. This place is very fond of people opting for a quiet comfortable stay in the villa. The villa is located in the residential villas complex and has round the clock security. Free secured parking for cars and scooters. Maid service is provided 1 time per week (cleaning, towels and bed linen change with maintenance of pool and garden).
