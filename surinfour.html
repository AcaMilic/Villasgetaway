Surin 4

This 4 bedroom, 4 bath luxury villa is the perfect getaway for a family or a group of friends wanting to relax and enjoy all that Phuket has to offer. Featuring leading-edge design and built to exceptionally high standards, the villa is one of 5 exclusively designed properties on a secure private development, with an on-site management office taking care of the estate and maintenance including the pools and gardens.


Decoration and design is contemporary modern Thai, the living accommodation features a blend of solid wood and terrazzo flooring, while black fired-slate is used extensively in the bathrooms. Guests will feel comfortable and welcome with the villas amenities.


The villa has an internal area of 550 Sq.meters and a further 500 Sq.meters of terracing, gardens and balconies. Accommodation is laid out on 3 levels and offers adequate space to comfortably sleep and cater for 8 adults. All rooms in the villa have their own individually controlled air-conditioning.
