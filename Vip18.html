VIP 18

Villa is an ultra-exclusive hillside estate on Phuket’s tranquil northwest coast, only 20 minutes from Phuket Airport. Every villa and residence in this premier collection blends award-winning design with spectacular ocean views to create a sleek modern oasis for the discerning traveller.


Private swimming pools, chic indoor and outdoor living areas and lavish bedroom suites are complemented by first-class service from a team of staff dedicated to meeting every holiday need.


The soft sands and clear waters of Nai Thon, one of Phuket’s most stunning and least developed beaches, beckons, as does a wide choice of restaurants, from world-class fine dining to exceptional local cuisine.


Families seeking a peaceful tropical sanctuary, couples dreaming of a stunning wedding venue, and friends wanting a luxurious home base for exploring the island will find all their wishes fulfilled at villa.
